flare_number Unitless

An ID number, ymmddnn, e.g., 2042101 is the first flare found for 21-Apr-2002. These numbers are not time

ordered, e.g., flare 2042129 occurred before 2042104. Also all sorts of problems have been noted since 2010

has started, and the energy range has been changed, resulting in more than 100 flares in certain days.

Flare_ids for 2010 and beyond start with a 2 digit year, and there can be 3 digits for the flare number in a

given day e.g., 100601102.
kso_flare Kanzelhoehe (KSO) flare list

Purpose:
Flares observed in H-alpha by the Kanzelhoehe Observatory.
Description:

The list contains the following information for each flare:



observed begin of the flare

observed maximum of the flare

observed end of the flare

heliographic position of the flare

Type: area (importance) [S, 1, 2, 3] and brightness [F, N, B] of the flare
Parameters

kso_id HEC id

Event identification number (HEC internal number)

time_start UT

Observed beginning of flare.

time_start_m

Time modifier: E ...Earlier

U ...Uncertain

D ...Later

time_peak UT

Observed maximum of flare.

time_peak_m

Time modifier: E ...Earlier

U ...Uncertain

D ...Later

time_end UT

Observed end of the flare.

time_end_m

Time modifier: E ...Earlier

U ...Uncertain

D ...Later

latitude degrees

Heliographic latitude of flare

longitude degrees

Heliographic longitude of flare

long_carr degrees

Carrington longitude of active region.

optical_class

Combination of area (importance) [S, 1, 2, 3] and brightness [F, N, B] of the flare.
