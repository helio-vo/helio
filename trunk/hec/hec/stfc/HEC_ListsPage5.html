lcc_id HEC id

Event identification number (HEC internal number)

time_start UT

Start time

time_end UT

End time

pa_central degrees

Central position angle for the CME

pa_measure degrees

The position angle at which the height-time measurements are made (MPA for measurement position

angle).

Ideally, the MPA and CPA (central position angle) must be the same. However, some CMEs move nonradially so the two

do not coincide. Even though there is no CPA for a halo CME, there is an MPA, corresponding to the PA of the fastest

moving segment of the CME leading edge.

pa_width degrees

Position angle width of the CME

linear_speed km s^-1

the linear speed obtained by fitting a straight line to the height-time measurements made at the fastest

section of CMEs

speed2_init km s^-1

quadratic speed obtained by fitting a parabola and evaluating the speed at the initial time of the event

speed2_final km s^-1

quadratic speed obtained by fitting a parabola and evaluating the speed at the time of final height

measurement

value is calculated in the plane of the sky.

speed2_20r km s^-1

quadratic speed obtained by fitting a parabola and evaluating the speed at 20 solar radii

value is calculated in the plane of the sky.

acceleration m s^-2

Acceleration is obtained from the quadratic fit to the height-time measurements

value is calculated in the plane of the sky.
hi_cme_list STEREO Heliospheric Imager CME list

Purpose:
List of CME events identified using the STEREO heliospheric imager instrument (RALspace)
Description:

This list was compiled by Richard Harrison (RALspace) through careful inspection of the images obtained from the

STEREO HI1 cameras. As such it is a more detailed list of CMEs, linking each to the appropriate transients seen in the

"hi_event_list" where possible. The list contains information about direction and extent of each CME in terms of

solar position angle.



Since the process of visual identification of CMEs is time-consuming, this list is currently only complete for CMEs

occurring to the end of 2008.
Parameters

hi_cme_list_id HEC id

Event identification number (HEC internal number)

time_start UT

Start time
